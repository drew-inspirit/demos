Step 5 of 6

So how does it know a post is "gaming"?

A brand-new video gets uploaded. How does Instagram sort it?
Same unsupervised idea — on content this time. Instagram clusters posts by who watches and likes them. If the same users who love esports clips keep watching a new video, it lands in that group. No human tags each upload — user interactions reveal the categories.
Users and content are both clustered from behaviour — then matched to each other to build your feed.

What is unsupervised learning?

Find hidden structure in unlabelled data — the model groups by similarity; humans interpret the groups after.
