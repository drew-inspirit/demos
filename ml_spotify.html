Step 5 of 5

What is supervised learning?

Train on labelled examples → learn the pattern → predict the label for new data.
🏷️It needs labelled data — examples with known answers
🧠It learns the pattern between features and labels
🎯It applies that pattern to predict new, unseen data
💬 Quick check — Why can't Spotify predict your taste if you've never liked or skipped anything?No training data — without labelled examples, there's no pattern to learn from.
