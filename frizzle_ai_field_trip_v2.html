Stop 3 of 5 · 🔧 The Builder's Toolkit
Build or Bust.
Pick which modern AI concept is the driving factor behind each listed product!
🔧
AI as a Tool
Does one specific job when you ask it to. Input in, output out, done. No memory, no follow-up, no initiative. Like a really smart calculator you pick up and put down.
🤖
AI as an Agent
Takes a goal and works toward it on its own. Uses tools, remembers what it has done, and keeps acting in a loop until the job is finished. You give it a destination — it figures out how to drive there.
⚙️
AI as a System
Multiple AI agents working together, each handling a different piece of a bigger problem. One might research, one might write, one might check the work. All coordinated toward a single outcome.
PRODUCT 1 OF 4
📅 An AI that browses your calendar, reads your emails, and automatically schedules meetings without you lifting a finger.
🤖 AI as an Agent. It needs memory (your calendar), tools (email reader, scheduler), and a loop that keeps acting until the meeting is booked. A simple tool just answers one question and stops.
PRODUCT 2 OF 4
🌐 A translator button in a browser extension that converts any selected text to Spanish instantly.
🔧 AI as a Tool. The extension calls a translation API, gets the answer, and displays it. No loop, no memory, no coordination — just one clean function call.
PRODUCT 3 OF 4
💊 A drug discovery platform where 50 AI agents each simulate a different molecule, share results, and converge on the best candidate.
⚙️ AI as a System. Many agents coordinating together, each running their own loop, sharing results, orchestrated toward a single goal. Classic multi-agent system.
PRODUCT 4 OF 4
🎓 A tutoring app that watches you solve a math problem step-by-step, identifies exactly where you went wrong, and generates a custom mini-lesson just for that mistake.
🤖 AI as an Agent. It observes your work, reasons about your specific error, then generates a personalised response. That observe-reason-act loop is exactly what an agent does.
