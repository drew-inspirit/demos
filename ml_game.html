Step 6 of 6

What is reinforcement learning?

Learn the best actions through trial and error — guided only by rewards and punishments.
🎲Agent starts with zero knowledge and acts randomly
⚡Receives rewards (+) or punishments (−) for each outcome
📈Improves over many trials — no teacher, no labels
